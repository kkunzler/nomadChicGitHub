Welcome to NomadChic!

This website will help make sure you never forget anything on vacation, ever again. It'll ask you about yourself and give you a packing list made especially for you. Put in where you're going, how long you'll be there, and a few other things about yourself and you'll be prepared for an awesome vacation, hassle-free.

This is still a work in progress - we know! Feel free to contact us at blahblahblah@email.com with any issues or feature suggestions.

Click the "Add A Trip" button to begin your list.

Welcome to NomadChic!

We are four girls who attended / are attending Girls Who Code at Adobe in Seattle, the summer of 2015.

Girls Who Code is a 7-week long intensive learning classroom. Together, we've learned Python, Scratch, HTML, CSS, Processing Javascript, jQuery, and other API's including Mapbox and Tabletop. We learned basic logic structures, starting with loops and progressing all the way to dictionaries and libraries.

We worked in groups and individually and we created games, websites, and random thing generators. We learned the value of self-confidence, basic public speaking skills, and how to work well in a team.

Each Girls Who Code participant received a mentor, and met with her weekly for one-on-one sessions. We set goals, talked about our passions, and thought about our futures.
