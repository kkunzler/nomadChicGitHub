Welcome to NomadChic!

This website will help make sure you never forget a darned thing on vacation, ever again. It'll ask you about yourself and give you a packing list made especially for you. Put in where you're going, how long you'll be there, and a few other things about yourself and you'll be prepared for an awesome vacation, hassle-free.

This is still a work in progress - we know! Feel free to contact us at blahblahblah@gmail.com with any issues or feature suggestions.

Click the "Add A Trip" button to begin your list. Don't worry - we'll be here when you get back.
